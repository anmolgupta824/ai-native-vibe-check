I built an AI agent that reads 90 articles and picks the 25 worth reading.

Cost: $0.006 per run.

Here's exactly how it works:

1. Scrapes 5 sources (HN, Product Hunt, GitHub trending, RSS feeds, web search)
2. AI scores each article on relevance + quality
3. Generates a personalized digest with TL;DRs
4. Emails it to you every morning

The entire pipeline runs in 3 minutes.

I built it because I was spending 45 minutes every morning reading newsletters. Now I spend 5 minutes reading the digest and 0 minutes feeling guilty about unread tabs.

Stack: TypeScript, OpenRouter (GPT-4o-mini for scoring, Claude for summaries), Supabase, Resend.

Open-sourced it today. Link in comments.
